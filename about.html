About Me:

Mathematic background:    I am a post-doc at Uppsala Universitet. Having recently finished my PhD at the University of Aberdeen, under the supervision of Dr William Turner, funded by EPSRC.

Keywords of my research interest: Koszul algebras, quasi-hereditary algebras, representation-finite self-injective algebras, derived equivalence, stable equivalence, Auslander-Reiten theory.
My current research projects contain the following keywords:
Brauer graph algebras; 2-representation theory; higher Auslander-Reiten theory; simple-minded systems.

I took my undergraduate degree at University of Cambridge, matriculated at October 2006, and took the Part III CASM as a student under DPMMS. I wrote an essay on "Blocks with cyclic defect group" under supervision of Dr Stuart Martin, the essay can be found in the Maths page.

Personal background:    I was born in England but my family, who were originally from Hong Kong, moved back to Hong Kong when I was 2, and we moved back to the UK when I was 16.

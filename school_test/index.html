講演予定者（講演順、敬称略）:  講演タイトル (based text)

: Triangulated categories review (Yang Ch.2 + others)
: Complexes, DG algebras, DG categories (Yang Ch. 3.1, 3.2, 4.1)
: DG functor, equivalences, representable functors, DG modules (Yang Ch. 3.3, 4.2)
: Homotpy category, derived category, Morita theorem (Yang Ch. 4.3, 4.4)
: Standard (tensor-Hom) functors and Rickard's theorem (Yang Ch. 5)
: Quasi-functors (Keller Sec.7)
: Koszulity duality (Keller's approach) (Keller's Sec.8)

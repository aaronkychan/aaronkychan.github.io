Evaluation
  • There is no examination. Grading is evaluated through homework assignments.
  • In each assignment, the highest scoring question will be recorded. (->4 recorded marks)
  • The next four highest scoring questions among all four assignments will also be recorded (->4 more recorded marks)
  • Final grading is determined by the average of these 8 recorded marks.
  • Grading scheme is as follows.
    • A : 85 ∼ 100%
    • B : 70 ∼ 84%
    • C : 50 ∼ 69%
    • Fail : 0 ∼ 49%

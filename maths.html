Info

Current Affiliation: Uppsala Universitet, Sweden (2014-?, Post-Doc, mentor: Prof. V. Mazorchuk)
Past Affiliation: University of Aberdeen, Scotland, UK (2010-2014, PhD, supervisor: Dr. W. Turner)
Research Interest:
- Tilting and derived category of finite dimensional algebras + related topics
- simple-minded systems and stable equivalences of self-injective algebras
- Brauer graph algebras + related combinatorics and geometry
- quasi-hereditary algebras and highest weight categories, standard Koszul algebras
- 2-representation theory of Mazorchuk-Miemietz
- higher Auslander-Reiten theory + related combinatorics
